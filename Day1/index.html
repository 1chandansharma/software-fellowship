Hera Pheri

Plot Summary

Hera Pheri (transl.Foul Play) is a 2000 Indian Hindi-language comedy film directed by Priyadarshan and written by Neeraj Vora, starring Akshay Kumar, Suniel Shetty, Paresh Rawal, Tabu, Om Puri and Gulshan Grover.

Main Cast

  • Sunil Shetty
